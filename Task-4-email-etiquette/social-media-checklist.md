# Social Media Do's and Dont's for College Students

## Do's

- Maintain a clear and professional profile, especially on platforms used for academics or careers.
- Be mindful or what you share online, as you posts can impact your future opportunities.
- Regularly review and update your privacy and security settings.
- Use social media to showcase your skills. projects, certifications, and achievements.
- Cross-check information from reliable sources before sharing it further.

----

## Dont's 

- Avoid posting harmful, disrespectful, or inappropriate content.
- Do not reveal sensitive personal details like your address, contact number, or financial information.
- Refrain from posting images or videos of others without their permission.
- Do not get involved in online conflicts; handle issues calmly or report them if necessary.
- Avoid excessive use of social media during study time or in situations where it is restricted.
